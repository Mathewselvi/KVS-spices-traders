Our Services

Comprehensive spice trading and processing services at our 20 Acre flagship facility

Spice Buying

We purchase premium quality cardamom, pepper, cloves, nutmeg, and dry cocoa directly from farmers at competitive prices, ensuring fair value for their produce while maintaining our quality standards.

Definition: The process of directly sourcing spices from local farmers at fair market prices, eliminating middlemen and ensuring quality through careful inspection and grading.

Cardamom Grading

Our expert graders sort and classify cardamom based on size, color, and quality, ensuring proper market categorization and optimal pricing for farmers while meeting international standards.

Definition: A specialized process where cardamom is categorized into different grades (Extra Bold, Bold, Medium, Small) based on size, color, aroma, and oil content to determine market value.

Farmer Produce Collection

We provide a central collection point for farmer produce, making it convenient for small and large-scale farmers to sell their harvest directly to us with immediate payment and fair pricing.

Definition: A centralized system where farmers can bring their harvest for assessment, weighing, and immediate payment, reducing transportation costs and time for local producers.

Cardamom Drying Unit

Our state-of-the-art drying facility ensures proper moisture content for cardamom, preserving its essential oils, natural color, flavor, and extending shelf life through controlled processing.

Definition: A controlled environment process that reduces moisture content in cardamom to 10-11% using calibrated temperature and humidity settings to preserve color, flavor, and aromatic compounds.

Grocery Store

Visit our on-site grocery store for premium spices and local products available directly from our farm to your kitchen, ensuring freshness and authenticity in every purchase.

Definition: A retail outlet offering freshly processed spices, local agricultural products, and specialty items with direct farm-to-consumer traceability for guaranteed quality and authenticity.

Wholesale Distribution

We supply high-quality spices to retailers, exporters, and bulk buyers across India and internationally through our established distribution network with quality certification.

Definition: Business-to-business supply of large quantities of graded and processed spices with quality certification, competitive pricing, and reliable logistics support for commercial clients.

Our Cardamom Processing

From harvest to package, discover our meticulous cardamom processing workflow that maintains quality and preserves natural flavors

1

Collection & Inspection

Freshly harvested cardamom capsules are collected from local farmers and undergo thorough inspection for quality, maturity, and consistency. Our skilled inspectors examine each batch to ensure only the finest cardamom enters our processing chain, maintaining our reputation for excellence.

2

Washing & Cleaning

The collected cardamom capsules are gently washed to remove dust, soil, and other impurities. This process is carefully managed to preserve the delicate essential oils that give cardamom its distinctive flavor and aroma while ensuring cleanliness.

3

Precision Drying

Our state-of-the-art drying facility uses controlled temperature and humidity to bring the moisture content to the optimal level (10-11%). This precision drying preserves the cardamom's natural green color, flavor compounds, and essential oils that make our spices exceptional.

4

Grading & Sorting

Dried cardamom capsules are meticulously sorted and graded according to size, color, and quality. Our expert graders use both traditional knowledge and modern standards to classify the cardamom into various grades for different market segments worldwide.

5

Packaging & Distribution

The graded cardamom is packaged in moisture-proof, airtight containers to maintain freshness and quality. From here, it's distributed to our retail outlets, wholesale customers, and export partners worldwide, carrying the distinctive KVS quality guarantee.
