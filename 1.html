Retail & Tour Booking Hub

The Anachal branch of KVS Spices & Traders is your gateway to Kerala's finest spices, authentic groceries, and the best local tour experiences. Conveniently located in Anachal town, we serve locals and visitors with top-quality products and travel services under one roof.

  • Premium Spices - Cardamom, pepper, nutmeg, and more
  • Fresh Groceries - From trusted local sources
  • Tour Booking Desk - Houseboats, cottages, safaris, and more
  • Money Exchange - Safe and convenient currency services
  • Local Products - Authentic Kerala specialties

Tour Booking Services

Book your perfect Kerala experience through our comprehensive tour services

Houseboat Tours

Experience Kerala's enchanting backwaters with our curated houseboat packages. Enjoy traditional cuisine, scenic views, and peaceful overnight stays on authentic Kettuvallams.

Hotel Bookings

Get the best rates at handpicked hotels and resorts in Munnar, Thekkady, and other popular destinations. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly options.

Cottage Stays

Enjoy homely comfort with our trusted cottage partners. Experience authentic Kerala hospitality in scenic locations with modern amenities.

Jeep Safaris

Adventure into the hills with guided jeep safari experiences. Explore hidden waterfalls, tea plantations, and wildlife sanctuaries with expert guides.

Money Exchange

Convenient, safe currency exchange for travelers at our desk. Competitive rates and secure transactions for all major currencies.

Custom Packages

Personalized tour packages tailored to your interests and budget. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences.
