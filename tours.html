Our Tour Packages

Choose from our selection of carefully curated tour experiences

Backwater

Alleppey Houseboat Cruise

Experience the tranquil backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat. Cruise through the network of lagoons, lakes, and canals while enjoying authentic Kerala cuisine prepared fresh onboard.

Backwater

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary & Backwaters

Combine bird watching at the famous Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary with a serene backwater cruise. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers wanting to capture Kerala's diverse ecosystem.

Wildlife

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary Jeep Safari

Explore the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary in a guided jeep safari. Spot elephants, tigers, leopards, and various deer species in their natural habitat in one of India's most beautiful tiger reserves.

Wildlife

Eravikulam National Park Tour

Visit the home of the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. This national park offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats and opportunities to spot various endemic species of flora and fauna.

Cultural

Kerala Cultural Experience

Immerse yourself in Kerala's rich cultural heritage. Witness traditional Kathakali performances, Kalaripayattu martial arts, and visit ancient temples to understand the spiritual roots of Kerala.

Cultural

Spice Plantation Tour

Discover the origins of Kerala's famous spices with a guided tour of local spice plantations. Learn about cultivation practices, medicinal properties, and the historical spice trade that shaped Kerala.

Adventure

Munnar Hill Station Trekking

Trek through the lush tea plantations and misty hills of Munnar. Various difficulty levels are available, from beginner-friendly walks to challenging mountain hikes for experienced trekkers.

Adventure

Backwater Kayaking Adventure

Experience Kerala's backwaters up close with a guided kayaking tour. Navigate through narrow canals, mangrove forests, and quaint villages for an intimate view of rural Kerala life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our tour services

What types of tours do you offer?

We offer a wide range of tours including backwater cruises, wildlife safaris, cultural tours, adventure activities, and customized multi-day packages. Whether you're interested in exploring Kerala's natural beauty, cultural heritage, or culinary delights, we have options for you.

How far in advance should I book my tour?

For regular tours, we recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance. For houseboat tours, especially during peak season (October to March), we suggest booking 1-2 weeks ahead. For customized multi-day packages, booking 2-4 weeks in advance is ideal to ensure availability of your preferred accommodations and activities.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (Indian Rupees), major credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. For international guests, we also offer currency exchange services at our Anachal branch. A deposit is typically required to confirm your booking, with the balance payable before the tour begins.

Is transportation included in the tour packages?

Most of our packages include transportation from a designated pickup point. For day tours, pickup from hotels in Munnar and surrounding areas is typically included. For multi-day packages, transportation between destinations is included, but airport transfers may be arranged at an additional cost. Details are clearly specified in each tour description.

Can you arrange special dietary requirements?

Yes, we can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other special dietary requirements with advance notice. Please inform us about your dietary preferences when booking your tour, and we will make the necessary arrangements with our tour operators and accommodation partners.
