Premium Spices & Tour Booking Hub

The Anachal branch of KVS Spices & Traders is your gateway to Kerala's finest spices and the best local tour experiences. Conveniently located in Anachal town, we serve locals and visitors with premium quality spices and comprehensive travel services under one roof.

  • Premium Spices - Cardamom, pepper, nutmeg, and more sourced directly from farms
  • Authentic Local Specialties - Traditional Kerala products and artisanal items
  • Tour Booking Desk - Houseboats, cottages, safaris, and custom packages
  • Money Exchange - Safe and convenient currency services for travelers
  • Travel Consultation - Expert advice for your perfect Kerala experience

Tour Booking Services

Book your perfect Kerala experience through our comprehensive tour services and local expertise

Houseboat Tours

Experience Kerala's enchanting backwaters with our curated houseboat packages. Enjoy traditional cuisine, scenic views, and peaceful overnight stays on authentic Kettuvallams with experienced crews.

Hotel Bookings

Get the best rates at handpicked hotels and resorts in Munnar, Thekkady, and other popular destinations. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly options, we ensure quality stays.

Cottage Stays

Enjoy homely comfort with our trusted cottage partners. Experience authentic Kerala hospitality in scenic locations with modern amenities and local charm.

Jeep Safaris

Adventure into the hills with guided jeep safari experiences. Explore hidden waterfalls, tea plantations, and wildlife sanctuaries with expert guides and comfortable vehicles.

Money Exchange

Convenient, safe currency exchange for travelers at our desk. Competitive rates and secure transactions for all major currencies with professional service.

Custom Packages

Personalized tour packages tailored to your interests and budget. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, we create perfect itineraries.
