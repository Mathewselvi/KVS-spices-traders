Anachal Retail Outlet – Local Touch, Premium Quality

Our Anachal retail outlet brings the best of KVS Spices & Traders to the local community and tourists alike. Established as an extension of our main 20 Acre facility, the Anachal branch offers premium spices, local products, and comprehensive tour booking services all under one roof.

Conveniently located on the main road in Anachal, our store provides easy access to the finest spices of Kerala along with authentic local products and personalized tourism services for visitors to the region.

What We Offer

Our Anachal branch provides a wide range of services and products

Spices Retail Store

A comprehensive range of spices, local products, and our premium spice blends available in one convenient location. We source the finest quality ingredients to bring the authentic flavors of Kerala to your kitchen.

Tour Booking Services

From houseboat adventures to jeep safaris, our tour booking service offers comprehensive options for exploring Kerala's natural beauty and cultural heritage. Our experienced staff can help you plan the perfect itinerary.

Farmer Partnerships

We pool products from nearby farmers to support local agriculture and ensure the freshest produce for our customers. Our direct partnerships with farmers ensure fair prices and sustainable agricultural practices.

Money Exchange

Convenient currency exchange services for international travelers visiting the Munnar region. We offer competitive rates for major world currencies to make your travel experience hassle-free.
