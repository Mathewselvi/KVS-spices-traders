Our Heritage

Founded in 1987 by the late KV Joseph, KVS Spices & Traders has grown from a small spice trading business to a respected enterprise spanning two locations in Munnar's spice heartland.

Today, after 38+ years in business, we're proudly managed by Abison Joseph, who continues his father's legacy of excellence, community support, and innovation in the spice trade.
